About Me

Blogilates

Hi! I'm Cassey Ho, an award winning fitness influencer and entrepreneur. I began my online fitness business at the early age of 20 while I was still in college. At this time I was working part-time as a pilates instructor, I decided to move from Los Angeles to Boston in the hopes of becomming a fashion designer. I wanted to provide my students that I was leaving behind content and so I recorded my very first YouTube video. Shortly after uploading I began receiving hundreds of requests for videos and subscribers to the channel skyrocketed. I now have more than 3 billion views on my YouTube channel and over 9 million subscribers.
